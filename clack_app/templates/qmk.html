Quantum Mechanical Keyboards (QMK) is a community based firmware development site for programming open source keyboards. This process can be pretty complicated if you do not have programming experience, but their site is very helpful in understanding the process. On the other hand, they have also developed a configurator that makes this process much easier for newcomers. They also have a very active dischord channel that you can get live help from. Happy clacking!

^QMK configurator This configurator has many preset options for a huge variety of keyboards on the market. You can further customize your layout with ease using their custom features. I would highly suggest reading up on the flashing process in the documentation before trying to flash you keyboard. If you think you have "bricked" your board, chances are you have simply done things out of order, so don't panic and remember you can visit discord for help!

^QMK documentation QMK has done an amazing job of providing documentation for how to successfully build firmware for your open source keyboard. While the process is fairly complicated, they do their best to break down each step so you can follow along. They also provide a great section on how to use Github to share projects, or grab files that you may need for your build. I would highly reccomend spending some time on this page before trying to build firmware yourself, or use the configurator. Remember to reach out if you get stuck!
